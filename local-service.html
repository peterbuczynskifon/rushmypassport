Rebuild the Local × Service Money-Page Matrix

The single largest structural opportunity. Convert the existing blog win pattern (11 city posts → 8,242 clicks) into conversion-ready money pages, capture displaced demand from competitors, and build the foundational hub-and-spoke architecture every other initiative depends on.

Three-Tier Local Architecture
A
City Hub
/passport-office/[city]/
A genuine local landing page differentiating on state law variation, regional processing times, the specific agency serving that city, major airports, and localized trust signals — replacing the current thin template.
B
Service × City Page
/passport-office/[city]/same-day-passport/
One page per service intent per city. ~7 services × 11 priority cities = 77 Tier B pages in the initial build. Each page answers ONE user intent, cleanly.
C
Supporting Content
/blog/ · /help-center/ · /faq/
Blog, help-center, and FAQ content flows internal links to Tier A and Tier B — it supports the money pages rather than competing with them.
